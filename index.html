About Myself

I'm a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Department of Information Engineering and Mathematical Sciences, at the University of Siena. I am the main developer and maintainer of PHAST Library, a high-level modern C++ programming framework for multi-core CPUs and NVIDIA GPUs. My research interests focus on hardware accelerators, architectural simulation, cross-platform high-level programming mechanisms, heterogeneous architectures, GPGPU, machine learning acceleration, parallel and distributed algorithms, haptic algorithms, and virtual/augmented reality environments.
