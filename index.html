About Myself

I'm a Researcher at the Institute of Science and Technologies "A. Faedo" (ISTI), an institute of the National Research Council of Italy (CNR).
I am the main developer and maintainer of PHAST Library, a high-level modern C++ programming framework for multi-core CPUs and NVIDIA GPUs.
My research interests focus on hardware accelerators, architectural simulation, cross-platform high-level programming mechanisms, heterogeneous architectures, GPGPU, machine learning acceleration, parallel and distributed systems, haptic algorithms, virtual/augmented reality environments, e-infrastructures, and virtual research environments.
